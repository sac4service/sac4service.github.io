Plantation


Sri Rama Krishna Sevasamithi, Bapatla as one of it's activities has been carring out afforestation in Bapatla. The plants are usually fetched from socieal forestry and taken care for couple of years in our permisses to mature them before taking them to actual location for plantation. Samithi also arranges transportation of tools and plants to actual site for plantation. Suryodaya Scoeity for Awakening Community (SAC) has been volunteering this program for plantation. SAC is geo-tagging contributor of this afforestation program. We thank William Junior College Management for extending their campus for afforestation and also water facility We thank APSPEB-Bapatla inviting us carryout afforetation in their campus.
We were starting jeo-tagging from the year 2018.
