We have chosen this village because of Child marriages, Dropouts from schools, Child Labour, Lack of information about Education and Health.
We are reducing these problems through several programs like Career Guidance, Health Camps,Skits regarding Child Marriages and Child Labour.

2017
  1. Our volunteers went to Maruproluvaripalem on (13th,14th,16th,18th) and gathered the Students who are interested in science exhibition and encouraged them to participate in science exhibition.
  2. Our volunteers went to Maruproluvaripalem on 4th March and interacted with students and found a dropout girl and interacted with their parents and motivated her parents for the future of their daughter.
  3. Our volunteers conducted classes for primary and secondary students on Mathematics and English.
  4. Our volunteers have also interacted with their parents for the development of their child’s education.
2016
  1. Our volunteers (about 20 members) went to MaruproluvariPalem and conducted survey on 1st and 2nd July. Problems we identified over there are:
    • Child marriages.
    • Education facilities are being provided up to 8th class only, and for higher studies they need to go to Bapatla, so parents are not willing to send their children (girls) to Bapatla.
    • No proper facilities for Bathrooms.
    • Parents addicted to Alcohol Consumption, effecting their livelihood.
    • Old people are having health problems like B.P, Diabetes etc.
    • Migration became a major problem for the education of their children.Economical problems affecting education of their children.
  2. From July 8th our volunteers (about 10 members) went to this village, conducting classes to primary and secondary school students (5th to 8th) after that we are interacting with villagers.
