Blood Donation

In order to help people in emergency SAC members collected a data base of about 700 students in the college which consists of their blood groups and contact numbers. Contact numbers of SAC members are given in various hospitals and medical shops nearby. In case of any call from needy the SAC members pass on the information to various students of the respective blood group to render their help.
