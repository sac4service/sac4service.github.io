ABOUT US

SAC is a formal committee of students formed at Bapatla Engineering college with a motto ‘NO CHILD WORKING, EVERY CHILD IN SCHOOL & LEARNING WELL’ established by N.Venu Gopala Rao on 3rd march 2008.
It was officially registered as a social welfare organization.0n June 3rd 2011(Regd No: 171 0f 2011 at Narsaropet).
SAC is now running successfully with 500+ volunteers working in the streams of education, child labor eradication, village programs, Beach cleaning, Plantation programs, Health camps and rural development activities.

Origin of SAC

It’s all started when our founder N.VenuGopal Rao celebrated his friend’s birthday in a government hostel. One among the children asked a question “Why everyone come there to distribute food, stationary but not spend time with them and help them in their studies and guide them” This made us to think how we can help them in a constructive and in a consistent manner. Initially with his class friends our founder started to spend the time and helps the students in their academics. In order to reach many needy students we form a “Students Action Committee” with an intention to curb the Child Labour and to provide and facilitate Education for all. Later we elaborate our Works on different Social Problems as do our Strength and Responsibilities increases.

With the union of other students, SAC team started helping the under privileged children (in and around Bapatla) by conducting educational classes. The whole union triggered the saying ‘Helping hands are better than praying lips’ into action.
